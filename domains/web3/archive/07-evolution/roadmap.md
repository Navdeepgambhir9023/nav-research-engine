# Roadmap

**Document ID**: 05.1
**Domain**: Evolution
**Status**: Draft

---

## Purpose

Provides a high-level view of the ROS's capability roadmap—planned milestones, feature development phases, and strategic direction. Aligns contributors around shared goals and tracks progress.

## Audience

- All contributors (for alignment)
- Stakeholders (for visibility)
- Architects (for planning)

## Roadmap Philosophy

> The roadmap tracks capabilities, not features. We build toward what the system *can do*, not what we *build*.

**Phase 1 Complete When**:
- [ ] All foundational docs approved

**Phase 2 Complete When**:

**Phase 3 Complete When**:
- [ ] Quality gates operate automatically

## Roadmap Maintenance

### Review Cadence
- **Monthly**: Progress check and adjustment
- **Quarterly**: Major review and planning
- **Ad-hoc**: Respond to significant changes

### Change Process
1. Propose change with rationale
2. Assess impact on dependencies
3. Update timeline if needed
4. Communicate to stakeholders
